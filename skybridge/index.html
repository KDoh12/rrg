About Sky Bridge

Sky Bridge is just one of the many beautiful sandstone arches that rest within the Red River Gorge. The arch itself is approximately 90ft long, 20ft wide, and 50ft from the ground. From the top of Sky Bridge, one can see breathtaking views of the surrounding cliffs and Clifty Wilderness.

Sky Bridge can be accessed via the well maintained, and partially blacktopped Sky Bridge Loop Trail at the end of Sky Bridge Road. The trail is a short and easy hike with it being only 0.8mi long. Despite the short hike, this trail offers stunning views of the Gorge and the cliffs surrounding Sky Bridge.

Tools used in this project

  • ArcGIS Pro was used to process lidar data and produce elevation derivatives.
  • Data is shown on Mapbox web map with geolocation services enabled.
  • Focal Statistics Tool was used to produce cliff heights

Data Used

  • Arches extracted from place names published by U.S. Board on Geographic Names
  • Elevation data extracted from lidar point clouds made available from KyFromAbove
  • 2016 NAIP Imagery used in Animation obtained from EarthExplorer
  • Photos of Sky Bridge taken by Me during visit to Arch
